Pro Tips for Beginners
🧱
Build, Don't Copy
Always type out code yourself. Never copy-paste from tutorials. Muscle memory is a real thing in coding.
📓
Maintain a Pattern Journal
After each solved problem, write: "This was a sliding window problem because...". Patterns stick better than solutions.
⏱️
Time-box Struggles
Spend max 30 minutes on a problem before checking hints. Struggling is good. Suffering in silence for 3 hours is not.
🔁
Spaced Repetition
Re-solve problems you found hard after 3 days, then 7 days. This builds real long-term retention.
🗣️
Talk Through Solutions
Explain your approach out loud like in an interview. If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it yet.
🎯
Quality Over Quantity
Thoroughly understanding 100 problems beats racing through 300. Focus on understanding the pattern, not just passing the test.
